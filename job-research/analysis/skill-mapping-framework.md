## Using This Framework

**For Each Job Application:**

1. **Map Requirements**: List all required and preferred skills
2. **Find Your Evidence**: Match to experiences in your profile
3. **Identify Gaps**: Be honest about what you lack
4. **Develop Strategy**: How to position gaps as opportunities
5. **Create Stories**: STAR format for each key requirement
6. **Prepare Responses**: Practice explaining your fit

**Example Workflow:**

```
Job Requirement: "2+ years Python experience"
‚Üì
Your Reality: "1 year Python, intermediate level"
‚Üì
Your Evidence: "Academic projects, self-study, Pandas experience"
‚Üì
Your Strategy: "Emphasize quality over quantity, rapid learning"
‚Üì
Your Response: "While I have 1 year of hands-on Python experience, 
I've rapidly progressed to intermediate proficiency through intensive 
academic projects and self-directed learning. I've applied Python to 
data analysis tasks using Pandas and am confident in my ability to 
quickly adapt to your specific use cases."
```
