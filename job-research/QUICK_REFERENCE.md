## ‚è∞ Interview Timing Guide

### Your Answers Should Be:

**"Tell me about yourself":** 60-90 seconds  
**STAR stories:** 90-120 seconds (max 2 minutes)  
**Technical explanations:** 30-60 seconds  
**Simple yes/no questions:** 15-30 seconds with brief example  
**"Do you have questions?":** Ask 2-3 questions (5-10 minutes total)

**Pacing Tips:**
- Pause before answering (shows thoughtfulness)
- Don't rush through examples
- Check interviewer engagement
- Ask "Does that answer your question?" if unsure
