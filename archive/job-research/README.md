# Job Market Analysis & Interview Preparation

## Overview
Comprehensive job research and interview simulation system for data analytics and AI roles.

## Structure

```
job-research/
├── README.md (this file)
├── job-postings/ - Saved job descriptions from Seek.com.au
├── analysis/ - Job requirement analysis and skill mapping
├── interview-prep/ - Role-specific interview questions and responses
└── simulations/ - Completed interview simulation records
```

## Workflow

1. **Research Phase**: Find and save 10+ relevant jobs
2. **Analysis Phase**: Map requirements to your profile
3. **Preparation Phase**: Develop targeted responses
4. **Simulation Phase**: Practice with different interviewer personas
5. **Iteration Phase**: Refine based on feedback

## Target Roles

Based on your profile (2nd year IT student with data analytics and AI career goals):

### Primary Targets
- Junior Data Analyst
- Data Analyst Intern
- Business Intelligence Analyst (Entry-level)
- Junior Business Analyst
- Analytics Graduate Programs

### Secondary Targets
- Junior Developer (Analytics focus)
- Data Engineer Intern
- AI/ML Intern
- Research Assistant (Data Science)

### Skills to Highlight
- Python, SQL, Database management
- Data analysis and visualization
- Problem-solving and analytical thinking
- Academic projects with data components
- Quick learner with strong foundation
