About the Company 

CleanPeak Energy is an Australian-owned renewable energy company. We build, own and operate a portfolio of large-scale solar, battery and thermal energy infrastructure – providing smart, reliable and cost-effective energy to our customers. 

About the Role 

We have an exciting opportunity for a Graduate Investment Analyst to join our North Sydney office on a full-time permanent basis. This is an entry-level role, and we will provide you with significant support and training to get up-to-speed on the renewable energy market.  

As part of the Commercial & Risk team, you will assist with the timely preparation of electricity retail pricing proposals, wholesale risk analysis and hedging strategies, and analysis of existing operating assets, with a focus on: 

Financial modelling for electricity pricing proposals in line with the business’s risk management framework. 

Analysis of the business’s electricity wholesale risk position. 

Financial analysis of the operating assets with a focus on profitability drivers and efficiency measures. 

Analysis for energy billing to ensure accuracy and alignment with commercial contracting. 

Financial modelling for internal commercial growth opportunities. 

Managing existing customer relationships and preparation of customer proposals for internal commercial growth opportunities. 

Assistance with board reporting and presentations. 

Developing and maintaining the business database of energy industry assumptions and forecasts. 

Role Requirements:  

A keen interest in Renewable Energy and the Australian Energy Industry and its market operations  

Bachelor’s Degree in Finance, Accounting, Commerce or similar 

Demonstrated ability with financial modelling, in particular MS Excel and other financial modelling or data processing tools 

Strong analytical skills with a high level of attention to detail 

Excellent written & communication skills  

Self-starter with the ability to adapt to a fast-paced environment
