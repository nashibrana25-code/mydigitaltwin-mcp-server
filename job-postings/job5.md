More about the role:

Responsible for gathering, interpreting and applying requirements, as IT Business Analyst you will play a key part in helping the Nursing Program achieve the Foundation’s mission as we take our care to all cancers.

This role provides the vital link between our IT and Nursing Program teams. You’ll work closely with stakeholders to enhance core business functions, improve processes, and outline data-driven insights to help deliver our nursing program strategy.

Key responsibilities of the role mean we are looking for applicants who can:

Identify opportunities, solve business challenges, and delivering impactful solutions
Develop functionality and capabilities across Salesforce platforms, data analytics and integration within the Nursing Program
Analyse and translate business processes into meaningful insights from a broad range of datasets to enhance product performance
Investigate problems, analyse options, and provide recommendations for the Nursing Program and IT teams
Model, analyse, and optimise business processes that are often repeated, ongoing, or predictable
Contribute to the development, facilitation, coordination and communication of initiatives and strategies to ensure consistency and adoption in the Nursing Program
What you bring to the role:

Our ideal candidate has 4+ years’ experience in business analysis and process improvement, is a clear communicator with strong technical report writing skills and experience working with projects.

Also key to success is:

A strong background in requirements gathering, project scoping, and stakeholder engagement
Proven ability to produce clear, high-quality documentation (eg. business and functional requirements, user stories)
Experience in process mapping, workflow optimisation, and data modelling
A collaborative mindset and a logical, analytical approach to problem-solving
Confidence in guiding stakeholders through change and innovation
