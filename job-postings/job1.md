## About the Role

Join a global leader Samsung within their Finance Division as a Junior Finance Analyst. This is an exciting opportunity to be part of a dynamic and high-performing team, contributing to strategic financial planning, analysis, and decision-making. The role offers a hybrid working model, with four days based in the office and one day working from home.

---

## Duties and Responsibilities

- Prepare and report aging inventory and variance analysis report, partnering with supply chain managers and operation team
- Prepare annual management plan, three-month rolling forecasts and monthly target planning
- Evaluate overhead costs trend and prepare analysis reports for forecast, target planning and month-end closing
- Assist with analysis of financial performance against forecasts and previous year
- Prepare and report monthly management reports
- Conduct the daily management and forecasting company's financial data
- Assist with preparation of various reports for HQ, RHQ and Senior Management
- Analyse financial data and provide recommendations to improve cost efficiency and financial performance
- Develop financial models, conduct benchmarking and process analysis
- Ad-hoc jobs as requested by Team Managers and Senior Analysts
